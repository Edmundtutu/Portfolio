About Me

Hi, I'm Edmund Tutuma, a passionate Full Stack Developer with expertise in building modern web applications. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I create efficient, scalable, and user-friendly solutions that solve real-world problems.

I have had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients who have consistently appreciated my enthusiasm and ability to deliver high-quality solutions. My experience includes collaborating with gynecology clinics to develop a custom machine learning model that predicts ovulation days, along with building an accompanying application.

Furthermore, I have been recognized for creating beautiful and highly functional virtual assistants. In addition, I developed a Java library that efficiently converts JSON to Java objects, which has been widely adopted by several developers.

My journey in software development began when I joined university at MUST as a software engineering student. Initially, I hoped to join Medicine, but my application was rebuked. I then decided to focus on working on myself with the best I could. I've since worked on various projects that have helped me develop a deep understanding of web technologies and best practices. I have over 2 years of experience in this field, continuously learning and growing with each project.
